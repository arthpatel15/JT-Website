Our School

Our school is one of the most popular schools in town. Our school building is very spacious and beautiful. Our school has a huge playground where I can play various outdoor games. I have many friends at our school where we study and play together. Our school teachers are very kind and caring towards everyone. We celebrate all national functions at our school with great pomp and show. Our school has a huge library where we can read books. Our school conducts physical education classes once every week. Our school has a science lab which is well-equipped. I love to go to school because I learn new things every day.
